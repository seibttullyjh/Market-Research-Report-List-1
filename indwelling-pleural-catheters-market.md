<p><p>Indwelling pleural catheters (IPCs) are medical devices used for the management of pleural effusions, allowing for continuous drainage of fluid from the pleural space. The increasing prevalence of conditions such as lung cancer, heart failure, and pneumonia, which can lead to pleural effusions, is driving the demand for IPCs.</p><p>Market growth analysis indicates that the Indwelling Pleural Catheters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.4% during the forecast period. Furthermore, ongoing clinical research is enhancing the understanding of IPC efficacy, which is likely to bolster adoption rates. Additionally, the expansion of healthcare infrastructure and a growing geriatric population are contributing factors, creating a robust environment for market growth.
