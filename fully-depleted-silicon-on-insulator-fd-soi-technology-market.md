<p><p>Fully Depleted Silicon-on-Insulator (FD-SOI) technology is an advanced semiconductor fabrication process that enhances performance and power efficiency in integrated circuits. By utilizing a thin layer of silicon on an insulator, this technology reduces parasitic capacitance, leading to improved transistor performance and reduction in leakage power. FD-SOI is particularly beneficial for applications requiring energy efficiency without compromising speed, making it ideal for devices in mobile, automotive, and Internet of Things (IoT) sectors.</p><p>The market for FD-SOI technology is witnessing substantial growth due to its advantages over traditional bulk silicon and other technologies.
