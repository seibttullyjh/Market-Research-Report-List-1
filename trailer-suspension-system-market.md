<p><strong>The Trailer Suspension System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Suspension Systems</li><li>Mechanical Suspension Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The trailer suspension system market is primarily divided into two types: air suspension systems and mechanical suspension systems. </p><p>Air suspension systems utilize air-filled bags to provide a smoother ride by adjusting to varying loads, enhancing comfort and stability. They offer improved weight distribution and reduce road impact. </p><p>Mechanical suspension systems, on the other hand, rely on traditional springs and dampers. These are simpler in design and often more cost-effective, providing adequate support and durability for heavy loads, albeit with less adaptability compared to air systems.</p></p>
