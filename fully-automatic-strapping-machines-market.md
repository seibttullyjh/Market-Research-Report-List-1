<p><p>Fully automatic strapping machines are essential in packaging and logistics, categorized into two main types: welding strapping machines and fastening strapping machines. Welding strapping machines use heat to bond plastic straps, creating a secure seal ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, fastening strapping machines employ mechanical mechanisms, such as clips or buckles, to secure straps, making them suitable for various materials and lighter applications. Both types enhance efficiency and reliability in packaging processes across industries.</p></p>
