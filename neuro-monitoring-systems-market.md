<p><strong>The Neuro Monitoring Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sub-Sensory Evoked Potentials</li><li>Brainstem Auditory Evoked Potentials</li><li>Visual Evoked Potentials</li><li>Somatosensory-Evoked Potentials</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Neuro Monitoring Systems encompass various types of sub-sensory evoked potentials that assess neural activity. Sub-sensory evoked potentials measure brain responses to stimuli, including Brainstem Auditory Evoked Potentials (BAEP), which evaluate auditory pathway integrity; Visual Evoked Potentials (VEP), assessing visual pathway functionality; and Somatosensory-Evoked Potentials (SEP), monitoring sensory pathways from the limbs to the brain. These modalities play crucial roles in diagnosing neurological disorders, guiding surgeries, and enhancing patient monitoring, thereby driving demand in the healthcare market.</p></p>
