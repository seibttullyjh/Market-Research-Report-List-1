<p><ul><li>Contact connector</li><li>Crimp connector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermocouple connectors market includes two primary types: contact connectors and crimp connectors. Contact connectors are designed for easy plug-and-play operation, allowing for quick connections and disconnections in temperature measurement applications. They ensure reliable electrical contact but may have limitations in environmental exposure. Conversely, crimp connectors involve a more permanent attachment method, where wire ends are secured using a crimping tool, providing robust connections suitable for harsh conditions. Both types cater to various industrial and laboratory needs depending on application requirements.</p></p>
