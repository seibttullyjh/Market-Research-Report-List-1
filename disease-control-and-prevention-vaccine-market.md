<p><strong>The Disease Control and Prevention Vaccine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Live-Attenuated</li><li>Inactivated</li><li>Recombinant Sub Unit and Toxoid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disease control and prevention vaccines can be categorized into four main types: Live-Attenuated vaccines use weakened forms of the virus or bacteria to induce immunity. Inactivated vaccines contain killed pathogens, stimulating an immune response without causing disease. Recombinant subunit vaccines consist of specific protein antigens from the pathogen, promoting immunity with minimal risk. Toxoid vaccines use inactivated toxins produced by bacteria to elicit an immune response, neutralizing the toxin's harmful effects while preventing disease.</p></p>
