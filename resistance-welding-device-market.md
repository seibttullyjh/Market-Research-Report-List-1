<p><p>The resistance welding device market comprises various welding techniques, including spot welding, seam welding, projection welding, and butt welding. Spot welding involves fusing metal sheets at specific points using heat generated by electrical resistance. Seam welding creates continuous joints by overlapping metal sheets and applying heat along the seam. Projection welding utilizes raised sections or projections on the workpieces to focus heat and pressure. Butt welding joins similar materials end-to-end, forming a strong connection without filler metals. Each method serves unique industrial applications.</p></p>
