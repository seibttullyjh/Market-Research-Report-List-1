<p><p>The pulverized fuel boiler market is categorized into three main types based on operational pressure and efficiency. Subcritical pulverized fuel boilers operate below critical pressure, offering moderate efficiency. Supercritical pulverized fuel boilers function at pressures above the critical threshold, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions. Ultra-supercritical pulverized fuel boilers take this further, operating at even higher pressures and temperatures, maximizing efficiency and minimizing environmental impact. Each type caters to varying energy demands and sustainability goals in power generation.</p></p>
