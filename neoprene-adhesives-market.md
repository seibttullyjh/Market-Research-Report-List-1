<p><p>The neoprene adhesives market is categorized into three main types: solvent type, emulsion type, and solvent-free liquid type. Solvent type adhesives are known for their strong bonding capabilities and fast drying times due to the presence of volatile solvents. Emulsion type adhesives offer a more environmentally friendly option, featuring water-based formulations that provide good adhesion while being safer to handle. Solvent-free liquid adhesives eliminate harmful solvents altogether, providing a durable bond while promoting safer working conditions and reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
