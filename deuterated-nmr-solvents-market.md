<p><p>Deuterated NMR solvents are solvents that contain deuterium, a stable isotope of hydrogen, which is extensively used in nuclear magnetic resonance (NMR) spectroscopy. These solvents are crucial for obtaining clear and interpretable spectra, as the presence of deuterium minimizes the interference that would arise from proton signals in typical solvents. The increasing demand for high-resolution NMR data in pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and academic research is driving the growth of the Deuterated NMR solvents market.</p><p>The market is characterized by innovations in solvent production and enhancements in quality.
