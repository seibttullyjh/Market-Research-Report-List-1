<p><p>The thermosiphon radiator market is primarily divided into two types: air-cooled thermosiphons and water-cooled thermosiphons. Air-cooled thermosiphons utilize air as the coolant, relying on natural convection to dissipate heat, making them suitable for applications with limited water access. In contrast, water-cooled thermosiphons use water for heat transfer, offering efficient cooling in various industrial and commercial settings. Both types leverage gravitational and thermal forces for circulation, promoting energy efficiency and effective temperature regulation in diverse environments.</p></p>
