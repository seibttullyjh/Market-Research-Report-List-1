<p><p>Reverse vending machines (RVMs) come in three primary market types: Non-Refillable, Refillable, and Multifunction. Non-Refillable RVMs are designed for single-use items, accepting and processing recyclable containers without the option for refilling. Refillable types allow users to return bottles or containers for reuse, promoting sustainability. Multifunction RVMs combine multiple features, accepting various materials like plastic, glass, and metal while offering incentives such as cashback or rewards. Each type contributes uniquely to recycling efforts and environmental conservation.</p></p>
