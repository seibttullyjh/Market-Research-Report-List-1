<p><p>The Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) microsphere market is categorized into two main types: hollow microspheres and solid microspheres. Hollow microspheres are lightweight particles that possess a hollow structure, making them ideal for applications requiring reduced density and improved buoyancy, such as in the aerospace and automotive sectors. Solid microspheres, on the other hand, are compact and offer excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for coatings, adhesives, and electrical insulation in various industries. Both types cater to distinct functional requirements in diverse applications.</p></p>
