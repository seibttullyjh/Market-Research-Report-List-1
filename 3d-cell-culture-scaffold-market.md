<p><p>The 3D cell culture scaffold market comprises three primary types: hydrogels, fibers, and other materials. Hydrogels are water-swollen networks that provide a hydrated environment, supporting cell growth and mimicking natural tissue conditions. Fiber scaffolds, made from various polymers, offer additional structural support and enhance cell signaling. The "other" category includes diverse materials like ceramics and metals, used for specialized applications. Together, these scaffolds enhance cell behavior and tissue engineering, fostering advancements in regenerative medicine and research.</p></p>
