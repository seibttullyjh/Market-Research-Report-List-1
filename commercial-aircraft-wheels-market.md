<p><ul><li>Main Wheel</li><li>Nose Wheel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial aircraft wheels market is primarily categorized into two types: main wheels and nose wheels. Main wheels are designed to support the majority of the aircraft's weight and withstand significant stress during takeoff, landing, and taxiing. They typically come in dual or single configurations, depending on the aircraft size. Nose wheels, on the other hand, provide steering capability and support the aircraft's front during ground operations. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety, performance, and durability in aviation operations.</p></p>
