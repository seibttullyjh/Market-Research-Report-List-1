<p><p>Ceiling-mounted lifts are categorized into three main types based on their operation: electric, manual, and hydraulic. Electric lifts utilize motors for easy operation, providing convenience and efficiency, ideal for frequent use. Manual lifts rely on human effort for movement, offering a cost-effective solution but requiring more physical exertion. Hydraulic lifts leverage fluid power to lift heavy loads, delivering strong lifting capabilities and smooth operation. Each type serves different needs, balancing functionality, cost, and user requirements in various environments.</p></p>
