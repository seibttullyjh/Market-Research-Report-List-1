<p><p>The PoE (Power over Ethernet) security camera market features three primary types: Bullet, Dome, and PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom). Bullet cameras are long and cylindrical, ideal for monitoring outdoor areas due to their long-range capabilities. Dome cameras have a discreet design, often used indoors or in retail settings, providing a wide field of view and vandal resistance. PTZ cameras offer advanced functionality with adjustable angles, allowing remote control for dynamic monitoring of large spaces, making them suitable for surveillance in varied environments.</p></p>
