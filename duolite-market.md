<p><p>Duolite market encompasses specialized ion exchange resins categorized primarily into cationic and anionic resins. Cationic resins attract and bind positively charged ions, making them ideal for applications in water treatment and metal recovery. In contrast, anionic resins target negatively charged ions, suitable for removing contaminants like nitrate or phosphate. Both types are critical in industrial processes, environmental management, and purification systems, offering efficient solutions for a variety of separation and filtration needs across diverse industries.</p></p>
