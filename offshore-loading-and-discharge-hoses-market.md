<p><p>Offshore loading and discharge hoses are crucial for transporting hydrocarbons between vessels and shore facilities. The market is divided into two main types: single core and dual core hoses. Single core hoses consist of a single layer designed for specific operational pressures, suitable for standard oil transfer. In contrast, dual core hoses incorporate two layers, enhancing flexibility and durability, making them ideal for higher pressure applications and environments with more demanding conditions, providing extra safety and reliability during offshore operations.</p></p>
