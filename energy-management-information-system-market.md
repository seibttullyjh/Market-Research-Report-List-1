<p><p>The Energy Management Information System (EMIS) market encompasses various technologies that optimize energy use and enhance operational efficiency. DCS (Distributed Control Systems) manage large, complex operations. Energy platforms facilitate data integration, while energy analytics offer insights for decision-making. Meter Data Management handles data from utility meters, and DRMS (Demand Response Management Systems) coordinate energy demand. Together, they enhance energy efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
