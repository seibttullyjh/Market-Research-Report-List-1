<p><p>Groove milling tools are specialized cutting tools used to create grooves in various materials. The market can be broadly categorized into external and internal grooving. External grooving tools are designed to cut grooves on the outer surfaces of components, providing features like seals, locking surfaces, or aesthetic designs. Conversely, internal grooving tools are used for applications inside hollow components, creating grooves for assembly, fluid flow, or structural reinforcement. Both types are essential in manufacturing for precision and functionality.</p></p>
