<p><strong>The Moored Buoy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Point Mooring (SPM)</li><li>Multiple Buoy Mooring (MBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Moored buoy market types include Single Point Mooring (SPM) and Multiple Buoy Mooring (MBM). SPM allows tankers to moor at a single buoy, enabling efficient loading or unloading of crude oil or products in deeper waters, often with reduced risk of vessel collision. In contrast, MBM involves multiple buoys and is ideal for handling smaller vessels simultaneously, enhancing operational flexibility. Both systems are crucial for offshore logistics, facilitating transportation in challenging marine environments while ensuring safety and efficiency.</p></p>
