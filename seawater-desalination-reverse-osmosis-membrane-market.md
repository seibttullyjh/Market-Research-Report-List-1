<p><strong>The Seawater Desalination Reverse Osmosis Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Composite Membranes</li><li>Asymmetric Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seawater desalination reverse osmosis membrane market is primarily categorized into composite and asymmetric membranes. Composite membranes are designed with multiple layers that enhance filtration efficiency and resistance to fouling, making them ideal for diverse seawater conditions. In contrast, asymmetric membranes have a gradient structure, combining a dense selective layer with a porous support layer, allowing for better water permeability and mechanical strength. Both types are crucial for producing fresh water from seawater, catering to the growing demand for sustainable water sources.</p></p>
