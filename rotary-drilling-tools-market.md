<p><ul><li>Augers</li><li>Drilling Buckets</li><li>Core Barrels</li><li>Temporary Casing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rotary Drilling Tools market comprises various types, including augers, drilling buckets, core barrels, and temporary casing. Augers are spiral-shaped tools designed for excavation, suitable for soft soils. Drilling buckets are used for hoisting soil from deeper depths, ideal for larger boreholes. Core barrels extract cylindrical samples of subsurface material, crucial for geological studies. Temporary casing provides support to the borehole during drilling, preventing collapse and maintaining structural integrity throughout the drilling process.</p></p>
