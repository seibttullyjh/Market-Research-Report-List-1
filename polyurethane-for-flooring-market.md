<p><strong>The Polyurethane for Flooring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Water-Based Polyurethane"</li><li>"Oil-Based Polyurethane"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane flooring market includes two primary types: water-based and oil-based polyurethane. Water-based polyurethane is favored for its quick drying time, low odor, and ease of cleanup, making it suitable for indoor applications. It forms a durable finish while retaining the wood's natural color. Conversely, oil-based polyurethane offers enhanced durability and a warmer, richer look but requires longer drying times and has a stronger odor. Both types provide excellent protection for various flooring surfaces, catering to specific preferences and needs.</p></p>
