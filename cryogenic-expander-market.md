<p><p>The cryogenic expander market is categorized into single-phase and two-phase expanders. Single-phase expanders facilitate the expansion of gases in a single phase state, typically focusing on efficiency and compactness for applications like liquefied natural gas. In contrast, two-phase expanders manage both liquid and vapor phases, enabling greater energy extraction from cryogenic processes, making them ideal for applications requiring high efficiency in cooling and refrigeration. Each type serves distinct operational needs within cryogenic applications.</p></p>
