<p><ul><li>Directional Antenna</li><li>Omnidirectional Antenna</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aviation antennas market primarily consists of two types: directional antennas and omnidirectional antennas. Directional antennas are designed to focus radio waves in a specific direction, enhancing communication range and signal strength for point-to-point systems, making them ideal for air traffic control and satellite communication. In contrast, omnidirectional antennas transmit and receive signals in all directions, providing broader coverage and reliability for applications like weather monitoring or ground communication. Both types are essential for ensuring safe and efficient aerial operations.</p></p>
