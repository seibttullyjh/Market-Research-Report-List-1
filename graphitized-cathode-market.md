<p><p>The graphitized cathode market consists of three primary types: semi-graphitic, graphitic, and graphitized. Semi-graphitic cathodes exhibit a mix of graphite and other forms of carbon, offering moderate conductivity. Graphitic cathodes are composed predominantly of graphite, providing high electrical conductivity and thermal stability, making them ideal for demanding applications. Graphitized cathodes undergo a specialized treatment process that enhances their performance characteristics, such as increased density and superior thermal and electrical properties, making them suitable for high-performance environments.</p></p>
