<p><p>The digital dental X-ray sensors market can be divided into two main types: intraoral and extraoral X-rays. Intraoral X-rays are used for detailed images inside the mouth, capturing specific areas such as individual teeth and surrounding structures, making them vital for diagnostics and treatment planning. Conversely, extraoral X-rays capture broader views of the jaw and skull, providing valuable insights for orthodontics and surgery. Together, these technologies enhance diagnostic accuracy and improve patient care in dentistry.</p></p>
