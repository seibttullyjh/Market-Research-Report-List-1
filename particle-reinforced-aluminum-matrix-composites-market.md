<p><p>Particle Reinforced Aluminum Matrix Composites (PRAMCs) encompass various types based on the reinforcing particles used. BC/Al utilizes boron carbide for lightweight and high-strength applications. Al2O3/Al employs alumina particles, providing excellent hardness and oxidation resistance. Other types may include a range of different materials for specific performance characteristics, addressing diverse industrial needs and applications in aerospace, automotive, and structural components.</p></p>
