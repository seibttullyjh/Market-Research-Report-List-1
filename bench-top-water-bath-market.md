<p><strong>The Bench-top Water Bath Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circulating Water Bath</li><li>Non-Circulating Water Bath</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bench-top water bath market is categorized into two main types: circulating and non-circulating water baths. Circulating water baths feature a pump that ensures even temperature distribution by continuously moving water, making them ideal for applications requiring precise temperature control. Non-circulating water baths, on the other hand, have stagnant water, providing simpler and more affordable solutions for less critical heating tasks. Both types are essential in laboratories for applications such as sample incubation, heating, and chemical reactions.</p></p>
