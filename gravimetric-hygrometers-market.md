<p><p>Gravimetric hygrometers measure humidity by assessing the weight change of a hygroscopic material as it absorbs moisture. The market is divided into pointer type and digital type hygrometers. Pointer type hygrometers use analog dials for readouts, offering straightforward visual interpretations. In contrast, digital type hygrometers provide precise measurements with numerical displays, often featuring advanced functionalities such as data logging and connectivity. Both types serve various applications, including HVAC systems, laboratories, and industrial processes, catering to diverse user preferences.</p></p>
