<p><strong>The Neurofeedback Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frequency/Power Neurofeedback System</li><li>Slow Cortical Potential Neurofeedback (SCP-NF) System</li><li>Low-Energy Neurofeedback System (LENS)</li><li>Hemoencephalographic (HEG) Neurofeedback System</li><li>Live Z-Score Neurofeedback System</li><li>Low-Resolution Electromagnetic Tomography (LORE-TA)</li><li>Functional Magnetic Resonance Imaging (fMRI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The neurofeedback systems market is diverse, encompassing various types, each with unique methodologies. Frequency/Power Neurofeedback systems focus on brainwave frequency modulation, while Slow Cortical Potential (SCP-NF) systems regulate slow brain wave changes. Low-Energy Neurofeedback Systems (LENS) target brain energy levels. Hemoencephalographic (HEG) systems monitor blood flow in the brain. Live Z-Score Neurofeedback adjusts real-time brain activity to normative data, and Low-Resolution Electromagnetic Tomography (LORE-TA) localizes brain activity sources. Functional Magnetic Resonance Imaging (fMRI) explores brain function through blood flow changes, offering insights into neurofeedback's therapeutic applications.</p></p>
