<p><p>The cationic photoinitiators market comprises three main types: onium salt photoinitiators, metal-organic photoinitiators, and organosilane photoinitiators. Onium salts, which generate reactive cations upon UV exposure, are widely used in coatings and adhesives. Metal-organic photoinitiators, combining metal complexes with organic components, offer efficient light absorption and reactivity. Organosilane photoinitiators, known for their stability and adhesion properties, are ideal for various applications, including composites and coatings. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, enhancing polymerization processes through cationic mechanisms.</p></p>
