<p><p>The silicon detectors market is primarily categorized into three types: pad silicon detectors, strip silicon detectors, and others. Pad silicon detectors feature a large, flat detection area, ideal for detecting high-energy particles and are widely used in imaging applications. Strip silicon detectors consist of multiple narrow strips allowing precise tracking and are commonly utilized in particle physics experiments. The "others" category includes specialized detectors catering to unique applications across various sectors, such as medical imaging and radiation monitoring, addressing diverse technological needs.</p></p>
