<p><p>The implanted infusion pump market is segmented into programmable and nonprogrammable categories. Programmable infusion pumps allow healthcare providers to customize medication delivery, adjusting dosages, timing, and rates according to patient needs. This flexibility enhances treatment precision, particularly for chronic conditions. In contrast, nonprogrammable pumps provide fixed infusion rates and dosages, offering simplicity and reliability without the need for complex programming. These pumps are suitable for patients requiring consistent, straightforward medication administration. Both types cater to varied patient requirements in therapeutic settings.</p></p>
