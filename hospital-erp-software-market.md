<p><p>Hospital ERP software is categorized into two main types: on-premise ERP and cloud-based ERP. On-premise ERP systems are installed locally on a hospital's servers, offering control over data and security but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based ERP systems are hosted remotely, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs, as updates and management are handled by the provider. Both options aim to streamline operations, improve patient care, and enhance resource management in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
