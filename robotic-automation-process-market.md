<p><ul><li>Rule Based</li><li>Knowledge Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Robotic Process Automation (RPA) is categorized into rule-based and knowledge-based types. Rule-based automation follows preset instructions to execute repetitive tasks, ideal for structured data and predictable processes, ensuring high efficiency and accuracy. In contrast, knowledge-based automation utilizes artificial intelligence, allowing systems to learn from experience and handle unstructured data, enabling more complex decision-making. This differentiation highlights the versatility of RPA in addressing various operational challenges, tailoring solutions for both predictable and adaptive environments within organizations.</p></p>
