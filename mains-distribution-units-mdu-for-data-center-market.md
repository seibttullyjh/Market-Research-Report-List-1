<p><p>Mains Distribution Units (MDUs) in data centers serve to distribute power effectively. Basic MDUs offer straightforward power distribution without monitoring capabilities. Metered MDUs provide real-time monitoring of power usage, enabling better energy management. Monitored MDUs enhance this with additional monitoring features, allowing for analysis of energy consumption patterns. Switched MDUs introduce control functionality, enabling users to turn power to individual outlets on or off remotely. Each type caters to different operational needs, enhancing efficiency and management in data centers.</p></p>
