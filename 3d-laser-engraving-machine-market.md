<p><p>The 3D laser engraving machine market comprises three primary types: Fiber Laser, UV Laser, and CO2 Laser. Fiber lasers are renowned for their efficiency and precision, making them ideal for metals and hard materials. UV lasers offer high-resolution engraving on a variety of materials, including plastics, with minimal heat affected zone, making them suitable for delicate applications. CO2 lasers are versatile and widely used for engraving organic materials like wood, glass, and acrylic, providing excellent detail and depth.</p></p>
