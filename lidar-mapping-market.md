<p><strong>The Lidar Mapping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid State Lidar</li><li>Mechanical/Scanning Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lidar mapping encompasses various technologies, primarily categorized into solid-state Lidar and mechanical/scanning Lidar. Solid-state Lidar utilizes a fixed array of laser emitters and detectors, offering compact designs, durability, and reduced costs, ideal for automotive and mobile applications. In contrast, mechanical/scanning Lidar employs rotating components to achieve a wider field of view and high-resolution 3D mapping, commonly used in surveying and autonomous vehicles. Both types serve distinct purposes in the growing Lidar mapping market.</p></p>
