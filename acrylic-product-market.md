<p><strong>The Acrylic Product Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Extruded Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic product market comprises two main types: cast acrylic sheets and extruded acrylic sheets. Cast acrylic sheets are produced by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, resulting in thicker, more durable sheets with superior optical clarity and resistance to impact. Extruded acrylic sheets, on the other hand, are created by forcing acrylic through a die, resulting in thinner, lighter sheets that are more cost-effective and easily manufactured. Both types serve various industries, including signage, automotive, and architecture, due to their versatility and clarity.</p></p>
